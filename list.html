    2. Lionel Messi, born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina, is considered one of the greatest footballers of all time. Known for his incredible dribbling, vision, and playmaking, he has won numerous Ballon d’Or awards. Messi spent most of his career at FC Barcelona, becoming their all-time top scorer, before joining Paris Saint-Germain in 2021 and later Inter Miami in 2023. He led Argentina to Copa América 2021 and the FIFA World Cup 2022 victories, cementing his legacy.


  2. CR7

    3. Cristiano Ronaldo, born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal, is a global football icon renowned for his goal-scoring, athleticism, and leadership. He has played for Sporting Lisbon, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and currently Al-Nassr. With multiple Ballon d’Ors, Champions League titles, and international triumphs, including Euro 2016 and the 2019 Nations League with Portugal, Ronaldo is celebrated as one of the sport’s greatest. His relentless work ethic and physical prowess have made him a record-breaking forward.
